📋 Project Overview

Institution: Jaypee Institute of Information Technology, Noida  |  Program: AI & Data Analytics

Student: Saanvi Grover  ·  Roll No. 2408030010

Binary classification model to predict graduate program admission using Logistic Regression on the Kaggle Graduate Admission dataset (500 records, 7 features).

🔬 Methodology

  • Cleaned 500 records — no nulls or duplicates found
  • Binarized target: Chance of Admit ≥ 0.75 → Admitted (1)
  • EDA: histograms, boxplots, correlation heatmap, pairplot
  • 75% train / 25% test split (stratified by label)
  • Logistic Regression (sklearn, max_iter=2000)
  • Compared vs Decision Tree and KNN — both underperformed
